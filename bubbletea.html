Bubble Tea


To create a delightful homemade bubble tea, begin by steeping 1-2 tea bags (or 1-2 teaspoons of loose tea leaves) of your choice, such as black, green, or oolong tea, in 1/2 to 1 cup of hot water. Adjust the steeping time to achieve your desired tea strength, then remove the tea bags or strain out the leaves. Sweeten the tea with 1-2 tablespoons of sugar, honey, or simple syrup, depending on your taste. While the tea cools, prepare tapioca pearls according to package instructions, sweetening them if necessary. Once the tea is at room temperature, combine it with 1/2 to 1 cup of milk or non-dairy creamer and a handful of ice cubes in a shaker or blender. Optionally, add fruit syrups, fruit puree, or flavored powders for extra flavor. Lastly, place the cooked tapioca pearls in the bottom of a glass, pour the tea mixture over them, and insert a wide straw to enjoy your homemade bubble tea, complete with chewy tapioca "bubbles."
