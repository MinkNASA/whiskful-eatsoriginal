Caprese


To prepare a delightful Caprese Salad, begin by slicing 3-4 ripe and flavorful tomatoes into rounds or bite-sized pieces. Arrange the tomato slices on a serving platter. Next, place about 8 ounces of fresh mozzarella cheese, also sliced into rounds or bite-sized pieces, evenly between the tomato slices. Tuck a handful of fresh basil leaves, preferably whole, in between the tomatoes and mozzarella. Drizzle a few tablespoons of high-quality extra virgin olive oil over the salad, ensuring it's evenly distributed. Optionally, add 1-2 tablespoons of balsamic vinegar for extra flavor. Season the salad with a sprinkle of salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Serve your Caprese Salad as a light and refreshing appetizer or side dish, showcasing the wonderful combination of ripe tomatoes, creamy mozzarella, and fragrant basil. Enjoy the simplicity and elegance of this classic Italian dish!
