Strawberry Smoothie


To whip up a delightful strawberry smoothie, start by gathering your ingredients. You'll need 1 to 1.5 cups of fresh or frozen strawberries, 1/2 to 1 cup of yogurt (Greek or regular, plain or flavored), and 1/2 to 1 cup of milk (dairy or a milk alternative). For a touch of sweetness, consider adding 1-2 tablespoons of honey or your preferred sweetener. If you want your smoothie extra cold, toss in a handful of ice cubes. For creaminess and a subtle banana flavor, you can also include a ripe banana in the mix. To elevate the taste, add 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract and for added texture and nutrition, consider a tablespoon of chia seeds. Blend all these ingredients together until smooth, pour into a glass, and savor the refreshing goodness of your homemade strawberry smoothie!
