Peanut Butter Sneakers


To whip up these delightful Peanut Butter Snickers Bites, start by unwrapping mini Snickers bars, one per bite you intend to make. Next, combine 1/2 to 1 cup of creamy peanut butter, 1/2 to 1 cup of powdered sugar, 2 tablespoons of butter, and optionally, 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract. Mix until you have a smooth and sweet peanut butter coating. Now, gently coat each Snickers bar with this peanut butter mixture and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. If you'd like, melt a handful of chocolate chips with a teaspoon of vegetable oil to make a chocolate drizzle. Drizzle the chocolate over the peanut butter-coated Snickers bites.
