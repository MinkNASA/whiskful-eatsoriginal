Minestrone


To prepare a hearty and comforting Minestrone soup, start by heating a few tablespoons of olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat. Add 1 finely chopped medium onion, 2-3 diced carrots, 2-3 chopped celery stalks, and 2-3 minced garlic cloves. Saut√© the vegetables until they become soft and fragrant. Next, add 2 diced medium-sized potatoes, 1-2 diced zucchinis, and about 1 cup of fresh or frozen green beans. Stir in a 28-ounce can of diced or crushed tomatoes, 1 can (15 ounces) of kidney beans (drained and rinsed), and optionally, 1 can (15 ounces) of cannellini beans (drained and rinsed). Pour in approximately 8 cups of vegetable broth and add a combination of fresh or dried herbs like basil, oregano, thyme, and bay leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the soup simmer for about 20-30 minutes until the vegetables are tender. In the last 10 minutes of cooking, add about 1 cup of small pasta and cook until it's al dente. Remove the bay leaves before serving. Garnish your Minestrone soup with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired. Enjoy this flavorful and nutritious Italian soup with some crusty bread on the side!
