Chicken Noodles Soup


To prepare a comforting pot of Chicken Noodle Soup, start by placing about 2 pounds of chicken (bone-in chicken thighs, boneless chicken breasts, or a whole chicken) in a large soup pot. Add 8-10 cups of water or chicken broth, 2-3 peeled and sliced carrots, 2-3 chopped celery stalks, 1 finely chopped onion, 2-3 minced garlic cloves, 2-3 bay leaves, and optionally, some fresh or dried thyme leaves. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to a simmer. Let it cook for about 30-45 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender. Remove the chicken from the pot and shred it into bite-sized pieces. Meanwhile, add about 8 ounces of egg noodles or your preferred pasta to the simmering broth and cook until they are al dente. Return the shredded chicken to the pot. Adjust the seasoning if needed. Serve your heartwarming Chicken Noodle Soup hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired. This classic soup is a comforting and satisfying meal, perfect for any time of the year.
