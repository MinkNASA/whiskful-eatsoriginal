Chocolate Milkshake


To make a classic chocolate milkshake, you'll need to blend together 2 cups of vanilla ice cream, 1 cup of milk (whole milk for creaminess or skim milk for a lighter version), and 2-4 tablespoons of chocolate syrup for that rich chocolaty flavor. Feel free to add a dollop of whipped cream, some chocolate chips or shavings, and even a maraschino cherry on top for an extra touch of indulgence. If you're feeling adventurous, you can customize your milkshake with toppings like crushed cookies, sprinkles, or a drizzle of caramel sauce. Once everything's in the blender, blend until smooth, pour into a tall glass, and enjoy your homemade chocolate milkshake!
