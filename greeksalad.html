Greek Salad


To make a delightful Greek Salad, begin by combining 2-3 peeled or unpeeled, sliced cucumbers, 2-3 diced ripe tomatoes, 1 small thinly sliced red onion, and 1/2 cup of pitted and sliced Kalamata olives in a large salad bowl. Optionally, add 1 sliced green bell pepper for extra freshness. Sprinkle approximately 4-6 ounces of crumbled or cubed feta cheese over the vegetables. For the dressing, whisk together 1/4 cup of olive oil, 2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar, the juice of 1 lemon, 1-2 minced garlic cloves, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, and salt and pepper to taste. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss everything gently to combine. If desired, garnish with fresh oregano leaves for added aroma. Serve your Greek Salad as a refreshing side dish or a light and satisfying main course, savoring the mix of crisp vegetables, tangy feta cheese, and savory olives in each bite.
