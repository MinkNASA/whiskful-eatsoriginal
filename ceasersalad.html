Ceaser Salad


To prepare a classic Caesar Salad, start by washing and chopping fresh Romaine lettuce leaves into bite-sized pieces and placing them in a large salad bowl. In a separate bowl, prepare the Caesar dressing by whisking together 1-2 minced garlic cloves, 1-2 anchovy fillets (optional for traditional flavor), 1 teaspoon of Dijon mustard, the juice of 1 lemon, a dash of Worcestershire sauce, and 1/2 cup of extra virgin olive oil until the mixture is smooth and creamy. You can also add 1-2 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese to the dressing. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Drizzle the Caesar dressing over the Romaine lettuce and toss until the leaves are evenly coated. Add crispy croutons and sprinkle grated or shaved Parmesan cheese on top. Serve your Caesar Salad as an appetizer or main course, and enjoy the perfect combination of creamy dressing, crunchy croutons, and savory Parmesan cheese.
