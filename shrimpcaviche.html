Shrimp Caviche


To prepare a delightful shrimp ceviche, start by placing 1 pound (450 grams) of peeled, deveined, and chopped fresh or cooked shrimp in a bowl. Pour 1 to 1.5 cups of freshly squeezed lime juice over the shrimp, ensuring they are fully submerged. Let them marinate in the lime juice for about 30 minutes to an hour, or until the shrimp turn pink and opaque, indicating they are "cooked" by the acidity. Drain any excess lime juice. Add 2-3 diced ripe tomatoes, 1 finely chopped red onion, 1/2 cup of chopped fresh cilantro leaves, 1-2 finely chopped jalape√±o or serrano peppers, 2-3 minced garlic cloves, and optionally, 1 diced ripe avocado for creaminess. Season with 1-2 teaspoons of salt, freshly ground black pepper, and, if desired, 1/2 teaspoon of ground cumin. Gently mix all the ingredients together. If you like, add cooked sweet corn kernels for extra texture. Refrigerate the ceviche for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Serve your shrimp ceviche chilled with tortilla chips or plantain chips on the side for a delicious and refreshing appetizer or light meal. Enjoy the zesty and vibrant flavors!
